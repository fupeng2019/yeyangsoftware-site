Privacy Policy

YeYang Clock / YeYang Tools

Effective date: 2026-04-17

The current version of YeYang Tools focuses on local alarms, reminders, and reliability guidance. This policy explains what information is processed in this version, why it is used, and how you can manage it.

1. Information we process

To provide alarm features, the app stores alarm details on your device, including time, label, repeat days, ringtone, challenge mode, vibration preference, and snooze duration.

To help you confirm whether alarms are more likely to ring reliably, the app reads notification permission status, exact alarm permission status, and related system setting states. These values are used only to show readiness guidance inside the app.

The current version uses Google AdMob rewarded ads so users can voluntarily watch an ad to unlock different background styles. We do not require account registration and we do not upload your alarm content.

2. How the information is used

Local alarm data is used only to create, edit, display, trigger, and restore your alarms.

Permission and system status data is used only to determine whether the alarm flow has the critical conditions it needs and to guide you to system settings when action is required.

The advertising SDK may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, or ad interaction information according to Google policies and your consent state in order to provide, limit, measure, and protect ad services.

If future versions add analytics, cloud sync, accounts, or other third-party services, this policy should be updated before those features are released.

3. Storage and sharing

In the current version, alarm data is primarily stored locally on your device and is not proactively uploaded to a developer server.

We do not sell personal information, and alarm time, labels, or challenge content are not uploaded to ad services for ad targeting.

Rewarded ads are provided by Google AdMob. Advertising data handling is governed by Google's terms, Google's privacy policy, and any in-app consent choices available to you.

When Android or iOS notification, exact alarm, or settings interfaces are used, they are invoked only to support reminder functionality.

4. Your choices

You can edit or delete alarm data in the app at any time, and you can disable notification or exact alarm permissions in system settings.

Where ad privacy options are required in your region, the app will show or provide access to Google's consent management flow.

If those permissions are turned off, some reminder behavior may not work as expected, especially in the background, on the lock screen, or after reboot.

5. Children, updates, and contact

The app is intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed to children. Minors should use the app with guidance from a parent or guardian where appropriate.

If you have questions or feedback about data handling or local data deletion, contact us at fupeng237@gmail.com.

When product features or data practices change, this policy should be updated accordingly and the latest published version will control.

This policy may be updated as the product changes. The current version focuses on local alarm functionality and uses Google AdMob rewarded ads to unlock optional background styles.

隐私政策

YeYang Clock / YeYang Tools

生效日期：2026-04-17

YeYang Tools 当前版本主要提供本地闹钟、提醒和可靠性设置引导功能。本政策用于说明当前版本会处理哪些信息、处理目的，以及你可以如何管理这些信息。

1. 我们会处理哪些信息

为了提供闹钟服务，应用会在设备本地保存你创建的闹钟内容，例如时间、标签、重复周期、铃声、挑战模式、震动开关和稍后提醒时长。

为了帮助你确认闹钟是否更稳定地触发，应用会读取通知权限、精确闹钟权限以及相关系统设置状态。这些状态仅用于在应用内展示“已就绪”或“需要处理”的引导信息。

当前版本接入 Google AdMob 激励广告，用于用户自愿观看后解锁不同背景风格。我们不要求你注册账号，也不上传你的闹钟内容。

2. 信息如何使用

本地闹钟数据仅用于创建、编辑、展示、触发和恢复闹钟提醒。

权限与系统状态信息仅用于判断提醒链路是否具备关键条件，并引导你前往系统设置完成配置。

广告 SDK 可能会根据 Google 的政策和你的同意状态处理设备标识符、广告标识符、粗略位置或广告互动信息，用于提供、限制、衡量和防滥用广告服务。

如果未来版本增加统计、云同步、账号体系或其他第三方能力，我们会在功能上线前同步更新本政策。

3. 信息存储与共享

当前版本的闹钟数据主要保存在你的设备本地，不会主动上传到开发者服务器。

我们不会出售你的个人信息，也不会将闹钟时间、标签或挑战内容上传给广告服务用于广告定向。

激励广告由 Google AdMob 提供。广告相关数据处理受 Google 服务条款、Google 隐私政策和应用内同意选项共同约束。

如果因系统能力需要调用 Android 或 iOS 提供的通知、精确闹钟或系统设置接口，这些调用仅用于实现提醒功能本身。

4. 你的选择与控制

你可以随时在应用内编辑或删除闹钟数据，也可以在系统设置中关闭通知权限或精确闹钟权限。

如果你所在地区需要广告隐私选项，应用会通过 Google 的同意管理流程展示或提供调整入口。

关闭相关权限后，应用的部分提醒能力可能无法按预期工作，例如后台、锁屏或重启后的提醒可靠性下降。

5. 儿童、变更与联系

本应用面向一般用户使用，并非专门面向儿童设计。如果未成年人使用本应用，建议在监护人指导下使用。
